NBA Live Match Visual Analytics

Autumn Systems has developed an advanced live match analytics platform for the NBA , utilizing cutting-edge computer vision technology to deliver real-time insights that enhance the fan experience and provide valuable data for teams, coaches, and analysts. By integrating AI-powered vision systems, we capture and analyze every movement on the court to generate deep, actionable insights.

Key Features & Capabilities:

Real-Time Player Tracking:
Utilizing computer vision to track player movements, positions, and actions in real-time, enabling precise analysis of player performance and team dynamics.

Shot & Pass Recognition:
AI algorithms automatically identify and categorize shots, assists, and turnovers, providing instant stats on offensive and defensive strategies.

Player Efficiency Metrics:
Our platform calculates player efficiency metrics, such as points per possession, shot accuracy, and defensive impact, giving users deeper insights into individual player performance.
